The Bannari Amman Institute of Technology (BIT), also known as BIT Sathy, is an autonomous engineering college located in Sathyamangalam, Erode, Tamil Nadu. Established in 1996, it is affiliated with Anna University and accredited by NAAC with an A+ grade. The campus is noted for its scenic location on the banks of the Bhavani River. Academic Programmes & Admission
BIT offers undergraduate (BE/BTech), postgraduate (ME/MTech, MBA), and doctoral (PhD) programs. BTech/BE Admissions: Primarily through TNEA (Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions) based on 10+2 marks. Management quota admissions may consider JEE Main scores. MBA/ME Admissions: Based on TANCET or GATE scores.
Fee Structure: BTech fees are approximately ₹2.8–₹3 lakhs total. The MBA programme fee is roughly ₹70,000. Placement Highlights
